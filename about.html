My Story

I’m a Computer Science student at Georgia State University. I’m interested in building apps that actually help people, even if the first version is small.

I like clean structure and good habits. One thing I’m learning is that it’s better to ship something simple and improve it, instead of trying to make it perfect on day one.

Outside of school, I enjoy fitness and keeping a steady routine. It sounds basic, but staying consistent makes everything easier — classes, projects, and personal goals.

My main focus right now is strengthening my fundamentals: HTML, CSS basics, programming structure, and writing code that doesn’t turn into a mess later.
